## 1. Identification: Is it really a Cockroach?

Before we discuss treatment, let's confirm what you are seeing. In North Carolina, we primarily deal with two types:

### The German Cockroach (*Blattella germanica*)

* **Size**: Small, about 1/2 inch long.
* **Color**: Light brown with two dark stripes on the pronotum.
* **Behavior**: Found in kitchens and bathrooms. They reproduce rapidly and are a major health hazard.

### The American Cockroach (*Periplaneta americana*)

* **Size**: Large, up to 2-3 inches.
* **Color**: Reddish-brown.
* **Behavior**: Often called "Palmetto Bugs", they prefer damp basements and sewers but will invade homes for food.

## 2. The Dangers of Ignoring the Problem

Many Wilmington residents ignore the early signs, leading to costly consequences.
> "The average cost of treating a severe cockroach infestation in North Carolina can exceed $500, plus the health risks of asthma triggers and bacteria." - *NC Dept of Agriculture*

Cockroaches are known vectors for Salmonella, E. coli, and other dangerous bacteria. Their droppings can also trigger severe biological reactions in children and pets.

## 3. Prevention & Treatment Strategies

* **Moisture Control**: Wilmington's humidity is a breeding ground. Use dehumidifiers in crawl spaces.
* **Sealing Entry Points**: Caulk windows and doors. Install door sweeps.
* **Sanitation**: Eliminate food sources. Store food in airtight containers and clean up crumbs immediately.
* **Professional Barrier**: DIY sprays often fail because they repel roaches into walls rather than killing them. Our perimeter defense stops them at the source using non-repellent transfer technology.
