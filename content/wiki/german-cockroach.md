# Pest Profile: German Cockroach

*   **Local Activity**: Year-round in Wilmington (Indoors)

## Identification
Small, light brown to tan, with two dark parallel stripes on the pronotum.

## Why they are dangerous
They carry salmonella and E. coli. Their droppings can trigger asthma attacks in children.

## Prevention
*   Seal all food containers.
*   Fix leaky pipes (they love Wilmington's humidity).
*   **Call Apex Guard**: Baits are more effective than sprays for this species.
