# Ants in Wilmington: A Complete Field Guide

**Analysis by Apex Guard Intelligence Team**

Ants are the most common nuisance pest in Wilmington, NC. From the structural damage caused by Carpenter Ants to the painful stings of Fire Ants, understanding what you are up against is the first step to reclaiming your yard and home.

![Ant Colony Inspection](/images/wiki/ants.jpg)
*Fig 1. Typical ant mound structure found in Wilmington soil.*

## 1. Common Species in Our Area

### Red Imported Fire Ant (*Solenopsis invicta*)

* **Danger Level**: High (Painful Stings)
* **Identification**: Reddish-brown, aggressive swarming behavior when disturbed.
* **Habitat**: Sunlit mounds in yards and parks.

### Black Carpenter Ant (*Camponotus pennsylvanicus*)

* **Danger Level**: Moderate (Structural Damage)
* **Identification**: Large (up to 5/8 inch), black.
* **Habitat**: Moist wood, often indicating a leak or rot in the home.

### Odorous House Ant (*Tapinoma sessile*)

* **Danger Level**: Low (Nuisance)
* **Identification**: Small, dark brown/black. Smells like rotten coconut when crushed.
* **Habitat**: Kitchens, searching for sweets.

## 2. Why They Invade

Ants enter homes primarily for:

1. **Food**: Sugar, grease, and crumbs.
2. **Water**: Leaky pipes or condensation.
3. **Shelter**: During heavy rains (common in Wilmington), ants seek dry ground indoors.

> "A single colony can house upwards of 500,000 workers. Killing the ones you see is only the tip of the iceberg."

## 3. Professional Control Methods

* **Baiting**: The most effective long-term solution. Workers carry slow-acting poison back to the queen.
* **Mound Treatment**: Direct application for Fire Ants.
* **Perimeter Defense**: Creating an invisible barrier around the foundation.
